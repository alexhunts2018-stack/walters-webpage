About This Site

We are a small tutoring company located at the heart of Oxford. Our goal is to assist and prepare students who want to pursue mathematics, engineering or a course with high utility of mathematics get offers from top universities. We believe that mainstream mathematical pedagogy has essentially been reduced to “teach to pass exams “, hence so much emphasis on the tricks, step by step memorization of solving mathematical problems. Consequently, more students are achieving high grades in the subject, yet come short when it comes to problem solving which is a major discriminating factor when applying to top universities. A paradigm shift to focusing on how to conceptualise and solve problems mathematically while getting computers to do the calculating should be the new normal in the pedagogy of mathematics.
